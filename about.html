ABOUT US

Located in the Sarona Compound. breakfast is a farm-to-table restaurant with a cozy and welcoming vibe. Chef Ran Shmueli, one of Israel’s most well-loved chefs, celebrates fresh local produce with creative ingredients, a big open-kitchen, a tabun (brick oven) and fresh fish, salads, specials cheeses and handmade-pasta and breads.
